Welcome
I study comparative political behavior with a regional focus on Latin America.

I am a Provost Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Political Science at the University of Southern California. My research examines political behavior, ideology, immigration, and public opinion using quantitative methods.

Selected themes

  • How ideology and partisan cues shape attitudes
  • Immigration and its political consequences
  • Attitudes toward policy and misinformation interventions
